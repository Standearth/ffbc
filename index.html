ELECTION 2024 Answer the Fracking Question!

Six weeks of action to push climate onto the election agenda and put every candidate on the spot about fracking and LNG.

Learn MoreRegister for Organizing Call

Disrupt climate denial to defend communities, end fracking and cool the climate!

B.C.’s next government will make crucial climate decisions in the next four years: Greenlight new fossil fuel extraction or keep gas in the ground? Build new pipelines or invest in a transition?

There’s no time for climate delay. We need to make every candidate, no matter their political stripes, feel surrounded by concern about fracking & LNG development. Together, we’ll put climate onto the political agenda, and force parties and politicians to respond to BC’s biggest climate problem: fracked gas.
