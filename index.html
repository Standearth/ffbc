B.C.’s next government will make crucial climate decisions in the next four years: Greenlight new fossil fuel extraction or keep gas in the ground? Build new pipelines or invest in a transition?

There’s no time for climate delay. We need to make every candidate, no matter their political stripes, feel surrounded by concern about fracking & LNG development. Together, we’ll put climate onto the political agenda, and force parties and politicians to respond to BC’s biggest climate problem: fracked gas.

The gas produced in B.C. is made up mainly of methane, a super-polluting greenhouse gas that’s 80 times worse for the climate than carbon dioxide.

If the B.C. government’s plan to massively expand fracking and LNG exports goes ahead, this one industry alone will blow B.C.’s climate targets. We’d condemn communities to more deadly floods, fires, and heat waves.

We have the solutions for a clean energy transition — but fossil fuel companies and their army of lobbyists have an outsize influence on B.C. politicians. We need to call it out and demand better!
